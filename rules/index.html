Guild Rules

The house rules. Mostly: be cool, raid prepared, let officers handle drama.

1. No drama

Zero tolerance, in any form: between guildmates, over loot, anywhere. Disagreements happen — handle them like adults and keep them out of guild chat.

2. Loot: High Ticket Items

High Ticket Items are all tier pieces, weapons, and trinkets. They're awarded by loot council, not rolled, on the priority Performance > Attendance > Guild Activity.

Subject to change.

3. Raid spots are competitive

New raiders trial before they're promoted to core. Trials don't get loot-councilled on High Ticket Items until they pass their trial.

4. Show up raid-ready

Fully gemmed and enchanted, consumables stocked, and on time.

5. Respect every member

No religious arguments, no slurs, no bigotry. Keep it civil.

6. Take issues to an Officer

If something's bothering you, message an Officer or @lohap directly. Don't air it in general chat, and don't spam.

7. It's a game

WoW is supposed to be relaxing. If the pixels are genuinely getting to you, step away — grab some water, take a lap, come back fresh.
