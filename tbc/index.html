The Leveling Route

Follow the path top to bottom. Each node is a milestone — the big circle is your level, the card tells you what to do and when to move on. Tick off each step (click the circle or the checkbox) and your progress saves automatically in this browser.

Step 1 / 17
0 / 17 done
  • Don’t turn in any quests with a faction until you’re honored (5999/6000 friendly).
  • When you reach honored, visit that faction’s quartermaster — there are good low-level options.

Hellfire Ramparts

  • Run Hellfire Ramparts.
  • Complete the quests outside until you get [Weaken the Ramparts].
leave when Honored with Honor Hold / Thrallmar

The Blood Furnace

  • Run The Blood Furnace once for the quests.
  • Do quests out in Hellfire Peninsula.
  • Consider grabbing [Mark of Conquest], [Mark of Defiance] or [Mark of Vindication] for easier farming.
  • Learn your new skills.
leave when Revered with Honor Hold / Thrallmar  ·  if not yet 62, stay till 62

The Slave Pens

  • Kill all slaves before the slavehandlers.
  • Turn in 10 [Unidentified Plant Parts] once — sell the rest.
  • Go to Fahssn and run repeatable quests until friendly with Sporeggar.
  • Grab [Oh, It’s On!] and [Stalk the Stalker] (good farming trinkets).
leave when Honored with Cenarion Expedition

The Underbog

  • Run it once for the quests.
  • Do quests out in Zangarmarsh.
  • Learn your new skills.
leave when Revered with Cenarion Expedition  ·  if not yet 64, stay till 64

Mana-Tombs

  • If not yet 65: farm Mark of Kil’jaeden / Firewing Signet (up to 220), or quest in Terokkar / Nagrand till 65.
  • Go to [Haggard War Veteran] in Shattrath and run the [A’dal] chain to pick Aldor or Scryers.
  • Turn in your [Mark of Kil’jaeden] / [Firewing Signet] until honored.
leave when Honored with Consortium

Nagrand Arena — Ring of Blood

  • See Gurgthock for the Ring of Blood chain.
  • Grab [I Must Have Them!] and [Bring Me The Egg!].
  • Visit Wazat for a blue cape in 2 quests.

Auchenai Crypts

  • Run it.
  • Learn your new skills.
leave when Ding 66

Old Hillsbrad Foothills

  • Run once to unlock access to The Black Morass.

Sethekk Halls

  • The Shadow Labyrinth key drops inside.
  • Sell all your [Arakkoa Feather].
  • Learn your new skills.
leave when Ding 68

Start Karazhan Attunement

  • Go to Deadwind Pass → Archmage Alturus.
  • Run the Karazhan attunement chain until you get [Entry Into Karazhan].

Shadow Labyrinth

  • Run at least once for the attune.
leave when Revered with Lower City

Shattered Halls Key

  • Go to Shadowmoon Valley and kill Smith Gorlunk.
  • Bring 4×[Fel Iron Bar], 4×[Mote of Fire], 2×[Arcane Dust].
  • Complete the chain to get the [Shattered Halls Key].

Arcatraz Key (start)

  • Go to Netherstorm → Nether-Stalker Khay’ji.
  • Run the chain from [Warp-Raider Nesaad] until [How to Break Into the Arcatraz].

The Steam Vaults

  • Run at least once for the attune.
  • Alternatively run The Shattered Halls if you aren’t revered with either.
  • Sell all your [Coilfang Armaments].
  • Learn your new skills.
leave when Ding 70

Hit Max Level

  • GZ! Go to Nutral in Shattrath and grab your flying mount.
  • Visit quartermasters — Honor Hold/Thrallmar, Cenarion Expedition, Lower City — for revered gear.
  • Aldor / Scryers: turn in items now till revered if you need to.
  • See Nether-Stalker Mah’duun & Wind Trader Zhareem in Shattrath for dailies.

Heroic & Attune Farm

  • The Botanica, The Mechanar (take [Harbinger of Doom]), The Arcatraz, The Black Morass.
  • Farm The Botanica till revered with Sha’tar.
  • Farm The Black Morass till revered with Keepers of Time.
leave when Fully attuned to T4 raids & every heroic dungeon

Raid Attunements

  • Nightbane: at honored with Violet Eye, return to Archmage Alturus; take [Medivh’s Journal] and finish the chain.
  • SSC: find [Skar’this the Heretic] in heroic Slave Pens → [The Cudgel of Kar’desh].
  • TK: Shadowmoon Valley → [Earthmender Sophurus] / [Earthmender Splinthoof]; take [The Hand of Gul’dan], finish the long chain, then visit A’dal for 4 trials.
  • BT / Hyjal: attunes aren’t available until phase 2.
