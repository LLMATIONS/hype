💎 Loot & culture

High-ticket loot (tier, weapons, trinkets) goes by loot council on the priority Performance > Attendance > Guild Activity. Everything else is rolled. We're competitive about raid spots and relaxed about everything else.

The full house rules live on the Rules page. Read them before you raid.

📣 Who we're recruiting

We're considering all applicants while we build toward a core 25. We're already deep on melee, so healers and ranged DPS stand out, but bring your best and we'll find you a spot. New raiders trial before they're promoted to core.
